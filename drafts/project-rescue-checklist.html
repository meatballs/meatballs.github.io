Checklist for Project Rescue

Date Mon 18 May 2015 Tags projects / rescue / agile
  • Is there a clear, unambiguous statement of what the project is attempting to achieve?
  • Does the project have the support and backing of the organisation's senior management team?
  • Is there a properly defined project board?
  • Is the level of risk and uncertainty understood and accepted by the project board?
  • Are the communcation channels between the project board and team defined and understood?
  • Is the project team sufficiently skilled and resourced?
  • Does the project have defined workflow processes and are they followed?
